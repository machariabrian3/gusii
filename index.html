Who We Are

Gusii Water & Sanitation Co Ltd

The Gusii Water and Sanitation Company (GWASCO) is a water and sanitation service provider in the Kisii and Nyamira counties Kenya. The company was established in 2006 and is responsible for the provision water supply and sanitation services to the residents the two counties.

About Us

Vision

To be the premier company in the provision portable water and sanitation services in Kenya.

Mission

To provide safe water and sanitation services efficiently and effectively to sustainably meet expectations the customers and other stakeholders.

Core Values

The core values are the fundamental principles that guide the behaviour every person or entity working with GWASCO. They should be practiced and embraced to create the organizational culture to drive GWASCO’s vision. The core values guiding GWASCO are:

  • Customer focus
  • Integrity
  • Gender Responsiveness
  • Pressionalism
  • Team work
  • Innovation
